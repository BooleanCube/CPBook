Anchors

Accessing other wiki files in the text bascailly easier navigation through the wiki files in the book Could also do External files and other links too. With anchors you can even access stuff like headers. You can even jump to headers in other files. [.[file#anchorheader]] you can even do mail links with "m.ailto:mail"
